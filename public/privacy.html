Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 30, 2024

AI Sales provides advisory tooling for web and app builders. This policy explains how we handle the information you share while using the app.

Information we process

  • Conversation data: prompts you send and AI responses we return.
  • Uploads: screenshots or files you provide for analysis.
  • Subscription details: name, email, and billing information supplied during checkout.
  • Usage metrics: anonymized analytics (page views, interactions) when enabled.

How we use it

  • Generate AI recommendations tailored to your app or website.
  • Improve product quality, triage issues, and monitor service performance.
  • Provide customer support and respond to requests.
  • Process payments and manage subscriptions via Stripe.

Data retention

Uploaded files are deleted after analysis unless you explicitly ask us to keep them for follow-up work. Conversation transcripts may be retained for quality assurance and support; you can request deletion at any time.

Third-party services

We rely on infrastructure and AI providers (such as OpenAI) and payment processors (Stripe). These services receive only the data required to perform their tasks. Each provider offers its own privacy policy.

Your rights

You can request access, correction, or deletion of your data by emailing support@aisales.com. If you are located in the EU/EEA or UK, you may also object to processing or ask for data portability.

Contact

Questions? Contact us at support@aisales.com.
