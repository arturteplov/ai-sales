Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 30, 2024

AI Trust provides advisory tooling for web and app builders. This policy explains how we handle the information you share while using the app.

Information we process

  • Conversation data: prompts you send and the responses we return.
  • Uploads: screenshots or files you provide for analysis. During this phase uploads are used to contextualise template-based recommendations and are deleted once the report is generated.
  • Subscription details: name, email, and billing information supplied during checkout.
  • Usage metrics: anonymized analytics (page views, interactions) when enabled.

How we use it

  • Generate heuristic recommendations tailored to your app or website.
  • Improve product quality, triage issues, and monitor service performance.
  • Provide customer support and respond to requests.
  • Process payments and manage subscriptions via Stripe.

Data retention

Uploaded files are deleted after analysis unless you explicitly ask us to keep them for follow-up work. Conversation transcripts may be retained for quality assurance and support; you can request deletion at any time.

Third-party services

We rely on infrastructure and AI providers (such as OpenAI) and payment processors (Stripe). Screenshot uploads are processed locally unless you opt into remote AI analysis; when shared, we only transmit the minimum data required to fulfil the request. Each provider offers its own privacy policy.
